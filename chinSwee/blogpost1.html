CHIN SWEE CAVES TEMPLE

Home About

This temple is located in the state of Pahang and a lot of travellers visit this beautiful area on their way to the Genting Highlands.If you look down from the temple, you will see glimpses of the highway covered in white soft cloud.

This temple was opened to the public in 1994. You will see different temple buildings as well as statues of Buddha peaking out of the forest trees.

Make sure to wear comfortable shoes because you need to do quite a bit of walking if you want to cover the whole area.
